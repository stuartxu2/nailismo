N°11Taste Education

How to read
a manicure.

A manicure is built from four variables: shape, finish, length, and signal. Configure your detail like a suit.

01 · Shape ·SQUOVAL·
Square
Squoval

Square feels sharper and more graphic. Squoval feels natural and easier for daily wear.

02 · Finish ·MATTE·
MatteGlossChrome

Matte is quiet. Gloss is polished. Chrome is high-signal.

03 · Length ·SHORT·
ShortMediumLong

Short is daily. Medium is styled. Long is editorial.

04 · Color ·SIGNAL·

Nude and gray are low signal. Black and navy are structured. Silver and chrome are high signal.

N°13Fearless FAQ

Questions
men actually
ask.

Confident. Direct. Practical. No apologies, no defensive language, no spa talk.

Need a person?

Email hello@nailismo.com for fit, removal, or styling questions. We reply in under 24h.

01

Will these look fake?

+
No. Nailismo focuses on shorter proportions, clean edges, and masculine finishes designed to sit naturally on men's hands.
02

Is this too feminine?

+
Not when the design language is right. Matte finishes, dark tones, architectural lines, chrome, and restrained contrast keep the manicure masculine and wearable.
03

Can I type, lift, or work with my hands?

+
Yes, for normal daily use. Use liquid adhesive for a stronger hold; use temporary tabs for short-term wear.
04

Can I wear them for one night only?

+
Yes. Use temporary tabs for events, parties, shoots, or first-time testing.
05

How do I know my size?

+
Start with a sizing range or starter set. Match each nail before applying. See the Fit System for a measurement guide.
06

How long do they last?

+
Temporary tabs are best for short wear. Liquid adhesive can last up to 7+ days depending on application, nail prep, and daily activity.
07

How do I remove them?

+
Soak and loosen gently. Do not rip them off. Most damage comes from forced removal, not from proper wear.
08

What style should I start with?

+
Start with Midnight French, Matte Charcoal, or Slate Nude. Clean, masculine, and easy to wear in real life.
