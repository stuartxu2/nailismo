# Nailismo Homepage Design Guideline

## 1. Project Purpose

Nailismo is not just a press-on nail store.

Nailismo is a culture-forward menswear and grooming brand introducing manicures as a new visible accessory category for men.

The homepage must function as:

1. A style manifesto
2. A cultural normalization tool
3. A product education page
4. A conversion landing page
5. A taste guide for first-time male buyers

The homepage should not feel like a beauty salon website.  
It should not feel like a cold tech product page.  
It should feel like a premium menswear editorial experience with strong ecommerce conversion mechanics.

## 2. Core Brand Positioning

### Brand Idea

Nailismo makes press-on manicures for men who treat style as detail, signal, and self-control.

### Positioning Statement

Nailismo is the new men’s accessory category for visible hands, finished outfits, and controlled self-expression.

### Brand Promise

Press-on manicures designed for men’s hands, built for daily wear, and styled to complete the modern masculine uniform.

### Brand Mantra

Not decoration. Detail.

### Category Reframe

Do not frame nails as beauty.  
Frame nails as menswear detail.

Nailismo belongs next to:

- Watches
- Rings
- Fragrance
- Sneakers
- Tailoring
- Haircuts
- Tattoos
- Eyewear
- Leather goods

Nailismo does not belong next to:

- Cute nail art
- Beauty salon pampering
- Glitter-heavy feminine styling
- Overly soft self-care language
- Generic cosmetic branding

## 3. Target Customer Groups

- Can I wear it for only one night?

## 4. Homepage Design Principles

### Principle 1: Show Before Explaining

Do not over-explain why men can wear nails.  
Show men wearing nails naturally in stylish situations.

### Principle 2: Treat Nails as Menswear

The product should be styled with watches, rings, cuffs, jackets, denim, hoodies, keyboards, espresso cups, steering wheels, and nightlife glassware.

### Principle 3: Make the First Purchase Feel Safe

First-time buyers need:

- Sizing reassurance
- Application simplicity
- Temporary wear option
- Natural fit proof
- Removal instructions
- Subtle style entry points

### Principle 4: Avoid Beauty Salon Language

Use structural, editorial, and menswear vocabulary.

Good words:

- Detail
- Signal
- Uniform
- Fit
- Finish
- Shape
- Texture
- Hardware
- Edge
- Restraint
- Control
- Polish
- Architecture
- Utility
- Daily wear
- Night out
- Starter set

Avoid overusing:

- Beauty
- Glam
- Cute
- Pretty
- Pamper
- Salon
- Feminine
- Self-care
- Diva
- Sparkle

### Principle 5: Build Desire and Reduce Friction at the Same Time

Every major section should answer one of these:

- Why should I care?
- Will this look good on me?
- Which style should I choose?
- Will it fit?
- Is it easy?
- Can I wear this in real life?
- What should I buy first?

## 16. Section 11: Fearless FAQ

### Section Title

Questions Men Actually Ask.

### FAQ Tone

Confident, direct, and practical.

Do not apologize for the product.  
Do not sound defensive.  
Do not over-explain gender norms.

### FAQ Items

#### Will these look fake?

No. Nailismo focuses on shorter proportions, clean edges, and masculine finishes designed to sit naturally on men’s hands.

#### Is this too feminine?

Not when the design language is right. Nailismo uses matte finishes, dark tones, architectural lines, chrome, and restrained contrast to make manicures feel masculine and wearable.

#### Can I type, lift, or work with my hands?

Yes, for normal daily use. Use liquid adhesive for stronger hold and temporary tabs for short-term wear.

#### Can I wear them for one night only?

Yes. Use temporary tabs for events, parties, shoots, or first-time testing.

#### How do I know my size?

Start with a sizing range or starter set. Match each nail before applying.

#### How long do they last?

Temporary tabs are best for short wear. Liquid adhesive can last up to 7+ days depending on application, nail prep, and daily activity.

#### How do I remove them?

Soak and loosen gently. Do not rip them off.

#### Are they reusable?

Some sets can be reused depending on adhesive type, wear time, and removal method.

#### Will they damage my natural nails?

Damage usually comes from forced removal, not from proper wear. Follow the removal instructions and never rip the nails off.

#### What style should I start with?

Start with Midnight French, Matte Charcoal, or Slate Nude. These are clean, masculine, and easy to wear.

### SEO and AEO Purpose

The FAQ should target natural search queries:

- Are press-on nails for men?
- Do press-on nails look fake?
- Can men wear fake nails?
- How do men size press-on nails?
- Can I lift weights with press-on nails?
- How long do press-on nails last?
- How do you remove press-on nails safely?

## 20. Typography

### Typography Direction

Use structural sans-serif typography.  
The type should feel architectural, masculine, and editorial.

### Recommended Font Pairings

Option 1:

- Headlines: Space Grotesk
- Body: Inter

Option 2:

- Headlines: Satoshi
- Body: Inter

Option 3:

- Headlines: Neue Haas Grotesk or similar
- Body: IBM Plex Sans

Option 4:

- Brand display: Ekster
- Body: Inter or Satoshi

### Typography Rules

Headlines:

- Large
- Tight line height
- Strong weight
- Minimal punctuation

Body:

- Clear
- Short paragraphs
- No decorative script fonts
- No overly soft lifestyle fonts

Buttons:

- Uppercase or title case
- Strong weight
- Clear action language

### Suggested Type Scale

Desktop:

- Hero headline: 72px to 110px

- Subheadline: 20px to 28px
- Body: 16px to 18px
- Small text: 12px to 14px

Mobile:

- Hero headline: 44px to 58px

Questions Men Actually Ask.

Will these look fake?

Is this too feminine?

Can I type, lift, or work with my hands?  
Yes, for normal daily use. Use liquid adhesive for stronger hold and temporary tabs for short-term wear.

Can I wear them for one night only?  
Yes. Use temporary tabs for events, parties, shoots, or first-time testing.

How do I know my size?

Soak and loosen gently. Do not rip them off.

Are they reusable?  
Some sets can be reused depending on adhesive type, wear time, and removal method.
